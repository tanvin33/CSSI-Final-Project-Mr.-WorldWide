How to Play!

Here's how to play Mr. Worldwide, The Snowman!

Play this word-guessing game to learn new words or a new language!

Choose from English, French, Spanish or Russian, at 3 difficulty levels.

Type the letter you want to guess on your keyboard.

Correct letters appear above blanks, while wrong letters are displayed on the left.

Need a hint? Click the sound icon the hear the word!

Tip: Change your keyboard language to type accented characters and use different alphabets.
